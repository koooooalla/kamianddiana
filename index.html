meme factory explores the phenomenon of human non-individuality. we believe that if everyone were truly unique, memes would not exist. a meme only lives because it finds an identical echo in someone else's mind. people believe they are independent, but they are actually a result of collective influence. this project is an invitation to see yourself as part of a larger human pattern, where even our smallest pains are shared and understood.
